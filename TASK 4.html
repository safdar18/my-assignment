Mahatma Gandhi

Mahatma gandhi leading the salt march.

1869: Born in Porbandar, Gujrat, India.
1888: Traveled to London to study law.
1915: Returned to India and joined Indian National Congress.
1930: Led the Salt March Protest against British salt tax.
1942: Launched Quit India Movement.
1947: India gained Independence.
1948: Assassinated in New Delhi.
